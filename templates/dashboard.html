Different Skin Types

Normal Skin

Characteristics: Neither too dry nor too oily, balanced complexion, small pores, minimal blemishes, and radiant appearance.

Dry Skin

Characteristics: Feels tight and rough, may be flaky or scaly, dull appearance, small pores, may show wrinkles more prominently. Feels less elastic.

Oily Skin

Characteristics: Shiny appearance, especially in the T-zone (forehead, nose, and chin), enlarged pores, prone to blackheads and breakouts.

Combination Skin

Characteristics: Oily T-zone (forehead, nose, and chin) and drier cheeks, can have enlarged pores in oily areas, and may experience breakouts and dryness simultaneously.

Different Skin Diseases

Desquamation disease

Desquamation refers to the shedding or peeling of the skin's outer layer. It can occur due to various factors, including infections, inflammatory skin disorders, and environmental factors like sun.

Nevi moles

skin growths caused by clusters of pigment cells. While most are harmless, some may become cancerous. Regular monitoring and dermatologist check-ups are important for detecting any changes

Diaper rash

Diaper rash is a common skin irritation in babies caused by prolonged exposure to urine or feces. It manifests as red, inflamed patches on the buttocks and genital area.

Acne

Acne is a common skin condition characterized by pimples, blackheads, and whiteheads. It is caused by clogged hair follicles, hormonal changes, and genetics.

Actinic Cheilitis

Actinic cheilitis is a precancerous condition causing dry, cracked lips and lesions. It is primarily caused by sun exposure.

Chapped feet

Chapped feet, or fissured heels, result from dry, cracked skin on the feet caused by factors like cold weather and low humidity.

Dyshidrosis

Dyshidrosis skin condition with itchy blisters on the hands or feet. Its cause is often unknown, but stress or allergies can trigger it.

Psoriasis

Psoriasis is a chronic autoimmune condition causing red, scaly patches due to rapid skin cell growth.It may involve genetics and triggers like stress.

Chondrodermatitis

Chondrodermatitis nodularis helicis is a painful lump formation on the outer ear due to pressure or friction. It commonly occurs who sleep on the affected side.

Rosacea

Rosacea is a chronic skin condition causing redness, visible blood vessels, and bumps on the face. Triggers include sunlight, stress, and certain foods.

Eczema

Eczema, or atopic dermatitis, is a chronic skin condition characterized by inflamed, itchy patches. Triggers include allergens and stress. Symptoms vary but commonly include redness and swelling.

How DermaMatch Works

Working of the Model Our system utilizes machine learning algorithms to analyze images of infected areas provided by users. Upon receiving a picture, our model identifies patterns and characteristics associated with various skin diseases. Based on this analysis, we determine the most probable condition the patient is suffering from. We then provide personalized recommendations for over-the-counter medications that can help alleviate symptoms. Additionally, if our model doesn't find a match or if the condition seems severe, we strongly advise users to seek professional medical advice from a doctor. Our goal is to provide immediate assistance and guidance for common skin conditions while also prioritizing the importance of consulting with a healthcare professional for accurate diagnosis and treatment
